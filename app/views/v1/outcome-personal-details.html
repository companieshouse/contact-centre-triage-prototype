A company is using my personal details without my permission

How to report an issue

You’ll need to use this online form to report the issue. We'll respond by email.

Before you send the form, read the Report a company using your personal details without your permission guidance.

If you need help

You can contact us in one of the following ways:

Webchat

Our webchat service is available between 8:30am and 6pm, Monday to Friday. We'll connect you to one of our advisors.

Phone

You can phone us if you need help, but the only way you can report a company using your personal details without permission is by using the online form.

Companies House
Telephone: 0303 1234 500
Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0300 373 0995
Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 6pm

Find out about call charges

British Sign Language (BSL) video relay service

To use the BSL video relay service you must:

  • first check you can use the service
  • go to the video relay service

The video relay service uses a free plugin. You may need to change the security settings on your device to install this.

Post

You can also write to Companies House, but you’ll usually get a faster response by using webchat or phoning us.
