Company accounts

Before you contact us

If you need to file your accounts online you can find out which service to use, go to our File your company's annual accounts with Companies House service.

You may find the answer to your query in the Filing your Companies House accounts guidance.

Find out if you can use your accounting software to file other types of accounts to Companies House.

If you still need help

You can contact us in one of the following ways:

Live chat

We'll connect you to one of our live chat customer service advisors. Live chat is available between 8:30am and 6pm, Monday to Friday.

Email

You can fill in the contact form to ask your query.

We'll respond by email, usually within 2 working days.

Phone

Telephone: 0303 1234 500

Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0300 373 0995
Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 6pm

Find out about call charges

British Sign Language (BSL) video relay service

To use the BSL video relay service you must:

  • first check you can use the service
  • go to the video relay service

The video relay service uses a free plugin. You may need to change the security settings on your device to install this.

Post

You can also write to Companies House, but you’ll usually get a faster response by using live chat, sending an online form or phoning us.
