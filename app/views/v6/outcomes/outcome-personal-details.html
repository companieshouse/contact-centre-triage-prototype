Reporting a company using my personal details without permission

How to report an issue

Use the online form to report if your personal details have been used without permission.

We'll ask about the details being used without permission and the company (or companies) using them. If you're reporting an address, we'll also ask for evidence showing that it's your address.

For more information, read the guidance on reporting a company using your personal details without your permission.

If you need help

The only way you can report a company using your personal details without permission is by using the online form to report the issue.

If you need help with the online form you can contact us in one of the following ways:

Live chat

We'll connect you to one of our live chat customer service advisors. Live chat is available between 8:30am and 6pm, Monday to Friday.

Phone

Telephone: 0303 1234 500

Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0300 373 0995
Monday to Friday, 8:30am to 6pm

Find out about call charges

British Sign Language (BSL) video relay service

To use the BSL video relay service you must:

  • first check you can use the service
  • go to the video relay service

The video relay service uses a free plugin. You may need to change the security settings on your device to install this.
